2025-03-14 datasheet facts used in this review (sources in datasheets/).
- HX711 (hx711.pdf): At AVDD=5 V, full-scale differential input is ±20 mV (Gain 128), ±40 mV (Gain 64), ±80 mV (Gain 32) per p1 description. Scales linearly with supply: at 3.3 V, ±13.2 mV (G128), ±26.4 mV (G64), ±52.8 mV (G32).
- FX1901 (ENG_DS_FX19_A13.pdf): Full-scale output 16–24 mV/V (nominal 20 mV/V), recommended excitation 5 V (p2). At 3.3 V, nominal FS ≈66 mV. Input resistance 2.4–3.6 kΩ; output resistance 1.76–2.64 kΩ (p2). Operating temp 0–50 °C (p2).
- RAK3172 (rak3172_datasheet.md): Supply range 2.0–3.6 V (Electrical Characteristics). No 3.9 V abs max is documented in the provided datasheet.
